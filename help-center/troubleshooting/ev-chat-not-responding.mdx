---
title: EV Chatbot Not Responding
description: Quick fix if EV doesn't reply to messages.
---

## Symptoms
- Messages sent but no response
- Loading spinner stays on

## Causes
- Internet connection issues
- Session expired

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh your browser">
    Reload `https://zefirchat.com` and log in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your connection">
    Make sure your internet is stable. Try opening another site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart the chat">
    Click "End Chat" then start a new session with EV.
  </Step>
</Steps>
