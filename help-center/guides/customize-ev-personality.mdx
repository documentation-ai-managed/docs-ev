---
title: Customizing EV's Personality
description: Personalize how EV responds in chats.
---

## Overview

You can adjust EV's style to match your preferred chat experience.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click the settings icon in the chat window with EV.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Personality">
    Select from options (Friendly, Witty, Professional) or enter custom prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Chat">
    Click "Save". EV replies using your selected personality.
  </Step>
</Steps>
